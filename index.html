Rotation-Unique Hexagon Fill Possibilities

Each hexagon has 12 parts: 6 inner triangles and 6 outer trapezoids (formed by an inner hexagon at half the radius). Every part is either white or black, giving 212 = 4096 possibilities. Rotational duplicates are removed, leaving 700 unique patterns. Bits 0–5 = inner triangles, bits 6–11 = outer trapezoids (label shows hex value).
